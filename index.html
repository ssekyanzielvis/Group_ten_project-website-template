Text Only

FoodDash is a comprehensive mobile application designed to simplify the food ordering and delivery process. With a focus on user-friendly interface and seamless integration with nearby restaurants, FoodDash aims to provide a delightful experience for users seeking quick and convenient meal options.

Developed by a dedicated team from the College of Computing and Information Sciences at Makerere University, FoodDash leverages advanced technologies to offer personalized meal recommendations, budget-friendly choices, and efficient delivery services. Our app ensures that users can easily navigate through various meal options, set their dietary preferences, and place orders with just a few taps.

FoodDash offers a variety of features that enhance the user experience. Here are some of the key functionalities:

  • Easy Meal Selection
    • Choose between breakfast, lunch, and dinner options.
    • Set dietary preferences based on past meal consumption.
    • Receive personalized meal recommendations.
  • Budget-Friendly Choices
    • Input your budget for meals.
    • Get suggestions for nearby restaurants within your budget.
    • Access exclusive discounts and offers.
  • Seamless Order Placement
    • Quickly place orders with just a few taps.
    • Track your order in real-time.
    • Receive notifications and updates about your order status.

By utilizing Firebase for the backend, FoodDash ensures robust and scalable performance, providing a reliable service for both users and restaurant owners.
